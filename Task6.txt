# Task 6:Comparison & Insights

1.Best Preprocessing Steps:
 
Lemmatization and stemming are techniques used in NLP to reduce words to their base or root forms.
Tokenization helps in breaking down text into smaller units such as words or sentences.
Lowercasing and stopword removal improved model performance

2.Best Vectorization:

Bag of Words(BoW) provides a clear and interpretable representation of text.
TF-IDF are easy to compute with minimal computational resources.
TF-IDF performed better than Bag of Words because it gives importance to meaningful words.

3.Best Model:

Logistic Regression gave highest accuracy and F1 score
Naive Bayes was fastest but slightly less accurate
Decision Tree overfitted the data

4.Trade-offs:

Logistic Regression → Accurate but slower
Naive Bayes → Fast but assumes independence
Decision Tree → Easy but unstable
